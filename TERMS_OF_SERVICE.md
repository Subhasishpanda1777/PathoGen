PathoGen is a public health monitoring platform that provides:

- **Medicine Finder**: Search for medicines and find affordable alternatives
- **Pharmacy Locator**: Find nearby Janaushadhi and other pharmacies
- **Symptom Reporting**: Report symptoms to contribute to community health monitoring
- **Health Risk Assessment**: Personalized health risk scores based on location and symptoms
